Section C:

Carfax indicates Clean Title (no total loss, Lemon Law, etc.)

Note: if the title is not clean, or if being sold with a retail disclosure, is not qualified as a Subaru Certified Pre-Owned Vehicle.

Section D:

  • Turbo models only: CID/CVN numbers from Techtile
  • Inspect vehicle for non-OEM parts. Vehicle must be returned to stock if non-OEM (non-performance enhancing) parts are evident.

Is vehicle stock? Yes No

If 'No', parts to be replaced on a separate sheet attached for the customer. Any modifications that cannot be returned to stock disqualify the vehicle.

Note: Nearest scheduled maintenance must be performed within 3 months or 2500 miles of service interval or if no record of service exists.

Section E - Hybrid Only:

  • Verify service is hybrid
  • Check active grill shutter operation
  • Check operation of start/stop system
  • Verify MFD shows battery charging
  • Check pedestrian approach system
  • Check battery cooling fan and duct

Section F - Tires:

  • Inspect and replace if damaged. Are all tires the same size/manufacturer/tread pattern? Check tire pressure, tread depth, same orientation, condition and meet OEM speed and load rating.
  • Tires have at least 5/32 tread left.

Vehicle Exterior:

  • Check spare/Mobility Kit
  • Check tool kit (all tools in kit)
  • Check jack
  • If 'No' note tires, tools, jack ordered
  • Check Section 'D2': repairs performed correctly
  • Check body panels (scratches through paint to primer must be repaired)
  • Check scratch free (scratches exceeding 1")
  • No holes/cracks in body and/or bumpers
  • No check paint. No chips larger than 1/4", no more than two chips (any size) per panel
  • Rust free exterior
  • Check door jams, rocker panels, hinges rust free, open/close smoothly all doors
  • Ding/dent free (none greater than 1/2" per body panel)
  • Check windshield (no chips/cracks)
  • Check side and rear glass (crack/chip free)
  • Check wiper arms (front and back)
  • Rear wiper blades (CPO requires replacement front and back (WA)
  • Check rear defogger
  • Check all door/window seals (excessive wear/damage)
  • Check headlight lenses/covers
  • Check tail light lenses/covers
  • Check fuel door opens/closes
  • Check fuel/cap/fuel cap operation
  • Check hood release operation
  • Check ignition door

Interior Inspection:

  • Ignition on, check chime
  • Ignition 'ON' check dash warning lights/gauges
  • Turn 'ON' rear defogger/check indicator
  • Check heater controls on all settings
  • Check for any abnormal smells/odors from vents
  • Check/replace cabin air filter
  • Check all vent operations (with heat or AC)
  • Check all inputs, rear AC
  • Check headlight/cruise control/turn signal stalk operation
  • Check odometer/trip settings
  • Check dash illumination control and display
  • Check Navigation System (WA)
  • Check head light leveler operation (WA)
  • Check Power Outlet
  • Turn headlights 'off', check bulbs
  • Turn 'ON' hazard flashers
